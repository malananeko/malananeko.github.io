The Creation of Japan

It tells how Izanagi and Izanami created the Japanese islands using a divine spear to stir the ocean. From the drops that fell, the first lands were born. They then created other gods, thus forming the sacred origin of Japan.

‹

Amaterasu Hidden in the Cave

Amaterasu hid in a cave after a fight with her brother Susanoo. When the Sun disappeared, the world fell into darkness and chaos. The gods held a party in front of the cave and lured her out with curiosity, restoring light to the world.

‹

Momotarō

Momotarō is a hero who was born inside a giant peach. He was raised by an elderly couple and grew up with great strength. Later he traveled to an island to defeat the oni who were stealing treasures. Along the way he made friends: a dog, a monkey and a pheasant, who helped him win.

‹

Princess Kaguya

The legend tells that an old man found a glowing girl inside a bamboo stalk. She grew up and became a beautiful woman named Kaguya. Many men wanted to marry her, but she rejected them all. In the end it is revealed that she comes from the moon and must return, leaving everyone in sorrow.
